### [2] 0fb81da4 · profbenjo@gmail.com · 2026-05-07T02:02Z · `/learn/d1000000-0001-4000-8000-000000000016`
> "when this page is expanded to show the smaller sub items the continue btn is hidden behind the bottom nav that shouldnt ever happen"

Cluster: **Layout — Continue button behind BottomNav**. When expandable sub-sections render (e.g. word chips in `PhraseBreakdown`, word family in `CollapsibleWordFamily`), extra content is added below the card. `SceneShell` has `overflow-x-hidden` which by CSS spec forces `overflow-y: auto`, making SceneShell the scroll container. The content zone had no bottom clearance for the fixed BottomNav (`fixed bottom-0 z-50`), so the Continue button ends up behind the nav even when scrolled to the bottom.

**Fix applied** in this commit: added `pb-20 lg:pb-0` to the content zone in `SceneShell.tsx` — 80 px clearance on mobile (matching BottomNav height), removed on desktop where BottomNav is `lg:hidden`.
